Hello! My name is Paarmita Pandey. I am a 5th-year PhD Candidate at The Ohio State University, Department of Astronomy. I am primarily a multiwavelength observational astronomer with broad interests.

I work with Prof. Laura Lopez and Prof. Todd Thompson on understanding cosmic-ray acceleration and transport in star-forming galaxies using Fermi-LAT gamma-ray data. I am also interested in time-domain astronomy. I work with Prof. Chris Kochanek and the ASAS-SN team on searching for failed supernovae in nearby galaxies. I also study extreme transients like Ambiguous Nuclear Transients.

Contact: pandey.176@osu.edu
